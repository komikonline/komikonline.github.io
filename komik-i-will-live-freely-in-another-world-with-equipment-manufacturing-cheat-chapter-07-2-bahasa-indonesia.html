7. Atlantic company has a manufacturing facility in brooklyn that manufactures robotic equipment for the auto industry ?


The country's automotive industry continues to experience rapid development from year to year which seems to have succeeded in becoming a magnet for the global automation or robot industry. Especially in the implementation of industry 4.0, the automotive industry requires a more effective and efficient production process in order to market products more quickly to the market.

This potential has also encouraged Universal Robots (UR) as a leading robot manufacturer from Denmark to participate in the national market. UR itself targets four-wheeled motor vehicle manufacturers in Indonesia, which continue to evolve in their production processes by utilizing technology.

Kukuh Kumara, Secretary General of the Association of Indonesian Automotive Industries (Gaikindo), admitted that automotive manufacturers in Indonesia have adopted robot technology or automation. Some parts of the work that already use robots include welding, painting, body, and so on.

"Indonesia's automotive industry is increasingly competitive, because it has used a lot of robot technology. However, the adoption process is done in stages. Because the economic value is still calculated when used in one stage of vehicle production," said Kukuh in a smart discussion at the Indonesian Automotive Journalists Forum (Forwot) entitled "Improving the Competitiveness of the Indonesian Automotive Industry Towards the Automotive Era 4.0" which took place in Jakarta, Thursday (15/8). ).

According to Kukuh, the Indonesian automotive industry is one of the strategic industries in Indonesia. Its contribution to Indonesia's 2018 gross domestic product (GDP) reached 1.76 percent or equivalent to Rp260.9 trillion. "The automotive industry was also included in the top 10 foreign direct investment in Indonesia in 2018 worth US$ 1 billion," he explained.

In terms of sales, from 2012 to 2018, car sales in Indonesia were at the level of one million units. This year, Gaikindo estimates its sales to reach 1.1 million units.

Meanwhile, Sakari Kuikka, General Manager of Universal Robots, added that Indonesia's market potential is promising. This is because the ratio of the use of robots in Indonesia's manufacturing industry as a whole is still low

15. The connection between World War I with awakening of nationalism in Indonesia?


Keterkaitan Perang Dunia 1 dengan nasionalisme di Indonesia adalah berdirinya organisasi pergerakan nasional di Indonesia.

PENJELASAN :

Perang Dunia 1 atau lebih di kenal juga sebagai Perang Besar dan Perang untuk mengakhiri Segala Perang adalah perang antara blok Sekutu (Inggris, Prancis, Rusia, Amerika Serikat) melawan blok Sentral (Jerman, Austria-Hungaria, Ottoman, Bulgaria) yang terjadi pada tanggal 28 Juli 1914 dan berakhir dengan kemenangan sekutu pada 11 November 1918 setelah di tanda tanganinya perjanjian Versailles.

Penyebab utama terjadinya Perang Dunia 1 adalah dibunuhnya pangeran Franz Ferdinand dari Austria di Sarajevo tahun 1914. Sedangkan penyebab umumnya adalah persaingan antara negara eropa dalam memperebutkan tanah jajahan dan Sumber Daya Alam (SDA) akibat sistem kapitalis yang banyak di gunakan oleh Bangsa Eropa saat itu.

Salah satu dampak dari Perang Dunia 1 adalah bangkitnya rasa nasionalisme yang memperkuat rasa nasionalisme di seluruh Eropa yang menyebar ke seluruh negara di dunia karena negara-negara diadu satu sama lain, termasuk Indonesia.

Perang Dunia 1 adalah langkah awal pergerakan nasional di Indonesia. Indonesia tidak banyak mengalami hal yang buruk selama Perang Dunia 1, sebaliknya Perang Dunia 1 justru menumbuhkan semangat nasionalisme dan berdampak pada berdirinya organisasi pergerakan nasional di Indonesia, di tambah dengan Perjanjian Versailles yang begitu mempermalukan bangsa Jerman yang berujung meletusnya amarah Jerman lewat naiknya partai NAZI dan akhirnya menjadi sebab utama Perang Dunia 2 yang membuat Hindia-Belanda (Indonesia) lepas dari tangan Belanda dan membuat Indonesia merdeka lebih cepat.
